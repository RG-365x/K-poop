K-Poop

K-pop has millions of fans—but TWICE as many haters. Whether it’s the flashy visuals, intense fandom culture, or repetitive beats, a lot of people just can't stand it.

So we created K-Poop: a parody project that pokes fun at the genre in the most *extra* way possible. This site is not for hardcore fans—it's satire, chaos, and unapologetic nonsense all in one place. Welcome to the madness!
Starting off the project with: Blackpink's How you like that

Funny fart sounds explode at every beat drop, and we've added weird sound effects to make it even more ridiculous.
